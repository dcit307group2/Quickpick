About Us

Quickpick is the first Ghanaian mobility super-app. We are offering a better alternative to every purpose a private car services – including ride-hailing, food and grocery delivery.
We believe that most errands in the city don’t require the use of a personal car. At Quickpick, we’re building a future in which people are no longer forced to buy a car to get around. Where people have the freedom to use transportation on demand, choosing whatever vehicle is best for each occasion.
Our products and services are designed to offer a reliable solution to the world’s logistical problems, doing so in an affordable, safe, and sustainable way.
